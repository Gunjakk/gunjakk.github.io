GUNJA HEADING PRACTISE

h1 heading ke liye use "h1 tag" exampal : < h1 align="center" > text </h1> ,text ko h1 heading me print krta h

h1 heading line ke liye use "hr tag " exampal:<hr /> line ko dikhata h

bold writing ke liye use "b tag" exampleee : <b > text < /b> , text ko bold me dikhata h

italic ke liye use "i tag" example : <i > text < /i> text italc me dikhata h
